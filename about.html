About the Author: Kellie Castro

Published by: Kellie Aveneth I. Castro

Published on October 28, 2020

Kellie Aveneth Castro, or simply Kellie or Kelle, is a 15 year-old student from Rizal, Philippines. She enjoys dancing, sleeping, reading, and basically everything nice. She writes essays or articles for school or for fun. Her recent essay, "The Issue on Diversity in STEM", is about the underrepresentation of the racially or ethnically different, as compared to white people, in STEM. In her essay, she provided possible solutions for the issue, in hopes of promoting diversity and ending discrimination in society, particularly in the above-mentioned field.
